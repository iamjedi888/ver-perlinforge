# Epic Contact Matrix

## Purpose

Separate the public Epic-facing lanes that matter to TriptokForge so we stop mixing:

- Unreal trademark/logo approvals
- Fortnite creator publishing and growth tools
- creator support and issue escalation
- broader collaboration or licensing asks

## The public lanes

### 1. Epic Account Services / Developer Portal app setup

Use this lane for:

- Epic account login for the website
- redirect URI setup
- application settings
- linked client and permission setup

Official public path:

- Epic Developer Portal / Epic Account Services configuration

Official source:

- `https://onlineservices.epicgames.com/en-US/news/player-authentication-with-epic-account-services-eas`

Important note:

This is the right public lane for the website login app. It is separate from Fortnite Creator Portal operations.

### 2. Unreal Engine trademark and logo use

Use this lane for:

- Unreal Engine logo use
- custom Unreal logo treatments
- Unreal mark placement in site or marketing materials

Official public path:

- trademark agreement and approval path:
  `trademark@epicgames.com`
- custom Unreal logo example review:
  `branding@unrealengine.com`

Official source:

- `https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/branding`

## 3. Fortnite creator operations

Use this lane for:

- island publishing
- teams
- analytics
- profile page
- sponsored row campaigns
- communities

Official public path:

- Creator Portal

Official sources:

- `https://dev.epicgames.com/documentation/en-us/fortnite/creator-portal`
- `https://www.fortnite.com/news/welcome-to-the-fortnite-creator-portal`

Important note:

Creator Portal is the official workflow for island submission and management. It is not presented by Epic as a branding or licensing portal.

## 4. Fortnite creator feedback and issues

Use this lane for:

- UEFN and Creator Portal issues
- developer workflow questions
- feedback on tooling and features

Official public path:

- Epic Developer Community forums
- dedicated issue forum
- Epic Developer Support

Official sources:

- `https://www.fortnite.com/news/welcome-to-the-fortnite-creator-portal`
- `https://dev.epicgames.com/en-US/support`

## 5. Creator support and account/program issues

Use this lane for:

- creator account issues
- support tickets
- escalation when self-service docs are not enough

Official public path:

- Epic Games Help
- Epic Support Assistant
- Contact Us / live chat / email ticket

Official source:

- `https://www.epicgames.com/help/en-US/c-202300000001645/c-202300000001672/a202300000014145`

## 6. Funding and strategic support

Use this lane for:

- project funding
- UEFN innovation support
- a stronger strategic relationship built around the project itself

Official public path:

- Epic MegaGrants

Official sources:

- `https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/megagrants`
- `https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/news/epic-megagrants-2025-recap-first-submissions-are-now-open`

As of March 15, 2026:

- Cycle 1 submission window is open until March 20, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET

## 7. What does not appear to have a public self-serve portal

I do not see an official public self-serve portal for:

- `TriptokForge x Fortnite` co-brand approval
- `TriptokForge x Epic Games` logo crossover approval
- direct strategic partnership intake specifically for a creator-built brand/community website

This is an inference from the public Epic materials above.

The practical result is:

- use Epic Account Services / Developer Portal for the site login app
- use the Unreal trademark lane for Unreal marks
- use Creator Portal for Fortnite publishing and growth features
- use support/contact forms to request routing for anything broader
- do not assume Creator Portal approval also grants co-brand or endorsement rights

## Recommended TriptokForge sequence

1. Keep the site login/app approval work on the Epic Account Services / Developer Portal lane.
2. Keep Unreal logo usage on the approved trademark path.
3. Keep Fortnite island operations inside Creator Portal.
4. Use Epic Support or Developer Support to request routing for any broader branding/collaboration ask.
5. If the business case is strong, apply for MegaGrants as the official project-support path.
6. Treat crossover branding as blocked until you have explicit written approval.

## Outreach framing

The strongest official-safe ask is not:

- "approve our crossover logo"

It is:

- "we have an accepted Unreal trademark agreement"
- "we are an active Fortnite/UEFN developer and studio"
- "here are the exact example materials"
- "please confirm whether this use is permitted"
- "if not, please route us to the correct Epic team for broader brand or partnership review"

## TriptokForge positioning summary

What TriptokForge is building:

- a Fortnite/UEFN-centered forge, community, channels, esports, and member platform
- a persistent creator-facing ecosystem around UEFN, Fortnite, and live community operations
- a studio/community hub that can drive discovery, education, promotion, and retention

Why that can matter to Epic:

- more UEFN creator visibility
- more creator education and onboarding
- more player/community touchpoints around Fortnite experiences
- higher-quality developer storytelling and ecosystem documentation
- possible amplification for launches, islands, communities, and creator growth features
