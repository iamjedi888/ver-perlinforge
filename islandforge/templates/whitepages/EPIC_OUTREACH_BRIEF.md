# Epic Outreach Brief

## Purpose

Give TriptokForge one clean, reusable brief for Epic-, Fortnite-, and Unreal-facing outreach so every submission or email says the same thing.

This is not a legal approval by itself. It is the working package we use when:

- asking for routing to the correct Epic team
- submitting Unreal trademark examples
- explaining the product to support or partner-facing contacts
- clarifying whether a broader co-brand or collaboration review exists

## What TriptokForge is

TriptokForge is a Fortnite and UEFN-centered platform running at `triptokforge.org`.

It combines:

- Epic-authenticated member accounts
- Island Forge generation for UEFN terrain, manifests, and Verse handoff packages
- live channels and community programming
- esports surfaces such as arena, leaderboard, and spectator concepts
- builder documentation and operating runbooks in WhitePages
- internal staff, moderator, and house-bot operations tooling

The platform is not trying to replace Fortnite or the Creator Portal.

It is a creator-facing studio and community layer around Fortnite, UEFN, and live ecosystem participation.

## Product goals

1. Make TriptokForge a high-quality home base for Fortnite players, UEFN builders, and esports/community operations.
2. Turn Forge into an easier bridge from creative ideas to UEFN-ready terrain and Verse handoff assets.
3. Improve creator discovery, education, and retention with channels, docs, walkthroughs, and community systems.
4. Build a member experience that feels premium, data-rich, and deeply connected to the Fortnite/UEFN ecosystem.

## Why this can help Epic

TriptokForge can help Epic and the Fortnite ecosystem by:

- increasing creator education around UEFN workflows
- creating more polished community touchpoints around Fortnite play and creation
- giving builders a stronger external operating layer for docs, tools, and presentation
- increasing discoverability for creator projects, live channels, and future islands
- giving serious creators a more durable studio-style surface than a single island page

## What we should not assume

- A Creator Portal approval is not the same thing as branding approval.
- An accepted Unreal trademark agreement is not automatically a Fortnite or Epic Games co-brand license.
- Epic login approval for the website does not mean access to private cross-game save data.
- A side-by-side `TriptokForge x Fortnite` or `TriptokForge x Epic Games` mark should be treated as blocked until Epic says yes in writing.

## Recommended sequence

1. Keep the site login/app approval on the Epic Account Services / Developer Portal lane.
2. Keep island publishing and creator growth on Creator Portal.
3. Keep Unreal marks on the existing Unreal trademark/branding path.
4. Use support or developer support to ask for routing when the ask crosses lanes.
5. If the ask becomes strategic, consider MegaGrants or a direct routing request that explains the business case.
