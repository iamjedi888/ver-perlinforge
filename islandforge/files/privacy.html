Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 — EuphoriÆ Studios

TriptokForge ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by EuphoriÆ Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform at TriptokForge.

Information We Collect

When you connect your Epic Games account, we collect:

  • Your Epic Games account ID and display name
  • Your Epic Games profile avatar (if available)
  • Public Fortnite statistics associated with your account
  • Your selected cosmetic skin preference for your member card

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or private Epic account data. We do not have access to your Fortnite locker or owned cosmetics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Epic Games OAuth2
  • Display your member profile card on the platform
  • Show your public Fortnite statistics
  • Maintain your session while you are logged in
  • Provide access to platform tools including Island Forge

Epic Games OAuth

Authentication is handled through Epic Games' official OAuth2 service. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to identify your account and display your public profile. We never store your Epic Games password. Your Epic account credentials are handled entirely by Epic Games — we only receive a secure token confirming your identity.

Data Storage

Member profile data is stored on our Oracle Cloud Infrastructure server located in the United States. We retain your profile data for as long as you maintain an account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Epic Games OAuth — account authentication
  • Fortnite-API.com — public player statistics (no personal data shared)
  • Oracle Cloud Infrastructure — server hosting

Cookies and Sessions

We use secure session cookies to keep you logged in. These cookies contain only a session identifier — no personal information is stored in the cookie itself. Sessions expire after inactivity.

Children's Privacy

TriptokForge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disconnect your Epic Games account at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered members of significant changes. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
