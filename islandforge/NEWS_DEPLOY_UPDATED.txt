# INTEREST-BASED NEWS SYSTEM - Deploy Guide

## What's New:

✅ **User Interest Checkboxes** - Users toggle what news they want to see
✅ **Positive Content Only** - Gaming, Anime, Cars, Art, Food, Nature
✅ **Optional General News** - BBC/Local news OFF by default (has ⚠️ warning)
✅ **Content Filtering** - Blocks war, violence, crime keywords
✅ **LocalStorage Preferences** - Saves user choices

---

## Interest Categories:

### Safe (Always Positive):
- 🎮 Gaming (Pokemon, Magic: The Gathering)
- 🎌 Anime (Crunchyroll, Anime News Network)
- 🇯🇵 Japan Culture & Ramen
- 🏎️ Cars & JDM (Speedhunters)
- 🎨 Art & Design
- 🌿 Nature & Conservation
- 🍜 Food & Baking
- 🔧 Restoration & Crafts

### Optional (May Have Negative Content - User Must Enable):
- 📰 General News (⚠️ warning badge)
- World news feeds

## How It Works:

1. User visits /news
2. Sees checkboxes for interests (Gaming, Anime, etc.)
3. Checks/unchecks what they want
4. JavaScript saves to localStorage
5. Sends POST to /api/news/latest with enabled categories
6. Backend filters feeds + negative keywords
7. Returns only positive news from chosen interests

---

## Default Behavior:

- First visit: All SAFE categories enabled (Gaming, Anime, Food, etc.)
- General News: OFF by default (user must enable)
- Content filter: Blocks war, violence, crime keywords
- Preferences: Saved in browser localStorage
